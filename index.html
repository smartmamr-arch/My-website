Surgical Specializations

Fields of Elite Surgical Expertise

Pioneering advanced surgical techniques in four key domains of pediatric surgery, delivering outcomes that transform young lives.

Anorectal & Pelvic Malformations

Specialized reconstructions for cloaca, imperforate anus, and complex pelvic anomalies. Pioneer of primary PSARP without colostomy in newborns.

PSARP Cloacal Repair Pelvic Reconstruction Continence Surgery

Neonatal Reconstruction

Pioneer in early-stage structural corrective surgeries for newborns, including esophageal atresia, diaphragmatic hernia, and abdominal wall defects.

Esophageal Atresia Diaphragmatic Repair Neonatal Defects Gastroschisis

Pediatric Urology

Complex reconstructive procedures of the genitourinary tract including staged hypospadias repairs using urethral plate substitution techniques.

Hypospadias Repair Bladder Reconstruction Urethral Plate Surgery Genitoplasty
